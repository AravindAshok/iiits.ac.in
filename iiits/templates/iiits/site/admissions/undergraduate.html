Under Graduate Admissions

Admission to Indian Institute of Information Technology is through Joint Seat Allocation Authority(JoSAA). All the interested candidates are requested to follow the admissions criteria explained on the JoSAA website.

Seat allocation for different categories of students will be as per JoSAA norms. The institute has no role in seat allocation process. For any other queries, please write to admissions@iiits.in
